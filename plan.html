Are you going to college? You need a plan! Think about the college search with your life goals in mind. How much education is required for the job you want? Do you need a four-year bachelor’s degree from a college or university? A two-year associate’s degree or certificate from a community college? College, an apprenticeship, or even the military are all good options. Explore your college options, plan college visits and think about college majors.
