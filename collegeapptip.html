Early Action

Early Action is a non-binding, unrestrictive admission plan. We aim to release decisions by the end of January. We may deny admission, offer admission, or defer the application. A deferred application will be evaluated again during the Regular Decision review. Students who are admitted under Early Action have until May 1 to make their deposit and reserve a seat in the first-year class.

After You Submit Your Application

Within five business days of submitting the Common Application to U.Va., you should receive an email from the uvaapplicationinfo@virginia.edu containing your U.Va. Computing ID, U.Va. Student System ID, and a link to the login page for the University of Virginia Student Information System.

Admission Decisions

Early Action decisions are posted at the same time. This will happen by the end of January. Regular Decision decisions are posted by the end of March. Keep an eye on the admission blog for announcements about the exact dates. On notification day, you can see your admission decision by clicking "view decision" at the bottom of the Student Center screen. You can also use the Student Center to update personal information (address, phone, email, etc.) as needed.
